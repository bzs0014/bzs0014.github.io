About

Research at the interface of biology and computation

I work on aquaculture genetics, fish genomics, genome editing, and bioinformatics, with a focus on translating genomic discoveries into practical breeding and functional research strategies.

My research interests include disease resistance, sex determination, reproductive biology, CRISPR-based genome engineering, and multi-omics analysis in aquaculture species.

Research Interests

From genomic discovery to practical aquaculture tools

01

Fish Genetics and Genomics

Genome-wide analysis, genetic mapping, QTL discovery, GWAS, sex determination, and trait-associated loci in aquaculture species.

02

Genome Editing in Aquaculture

CRISPR/Cas-mediated knockout, knock-in, regulatory editing, gene function validation, and genome engineering strategies in fish.

03

Disease Resistance and Immunogenomics

Genetic mechanisms underlying resistance to bacterial pathogens, mucosal immunity, immune-related loci, and host-pathogen interactions.

04

Bioinformatics and Multi-omics

RNA-seq, ATAC-seq, single-cell analysis, Hi-C/3D genome analysis, genome annotation, and computational pipeline development.

05

AI-assisted Biological Research

Applications of machine learning and AI tools in literature mining, gene-editing analysis, genomic prediction, and workflow automation.
